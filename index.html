About

Strange Depths is a three person indie game studio technically born in Michigan, USA but currently we have no definite location as we are spread across the world! Right now we are working on our first game "Life Light: Glyph" as I'm sure you've noticed! Having all met online relatively recently we're still getting to know eachother but the chemistry is there! We all have the goal of deepening our understanding of game development in every category and creating fun, engaging experiences where skill is valued over sheer strength.

"Life Light: Glyph" is a project that has a very long history of which you can learn about in our first devlog and YouTube video! Our current goal is to have a playable demo out by mid December of this year (2025) but we want it to be as good as possible so if we think it needs more time, we will do so.

Join us in our Discord server to witness our behind the scenes and participate in the playtesting! If you'd like to support us you can do so through our Kickstarter!
